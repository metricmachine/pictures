Team Top Hat is a dedicated team of Programmers, Voice Actors, Artists, Musicians, Writers, Puzzle Creators and Beta Testers here to bring you more Layton games.
We have two divisions, one that works to produce Layton games, and a division that creates the engine to produce games! 

One division, the programmers, work on our primary engine, the Layton Editor. Using Diabolical Box as a base, you can load data to create a new game! This division is what helps to create the mechanics that you'd otherwise have to recreate in another game engine.

The other division is made up of our creatives, the ones that bring new stories into Layton. With a combination of visuals, storytelling, and brain-teasers, we hope to bring our love for the Layton series to the next level: Making games in tribute of Level-5's founder, Akihiro Hino, and the late Puzzle Master, Professor Akira Tago.
